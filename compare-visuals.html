Visual Design Comparison

Compare the original clunky box designs with the new cleaner, more elegant alternatives. Same data, better presentation.

What Changed Across All Sections?

  • Removed heavy borders: Replaced 3px colored borders with subtle shadows and clean white backgrounds
  • Better visual hierarchy: Used typography (size, weight, color) instead of boxes to create structure
  • More white space: Let content breathe - reduced clutter and visual weight
  • Unified designs: Combined related stats into single cohesive visuals instead of competing boxes
  • Professional simplicity: Clean, minimal, elegant - let the data do the talking

Design Philosophy

The new designs follow these principles:

  • Less is more: Remove unnecessary visual elements
  • White space: Let content breathe
  • Typography first: Use size, weight, and color to create hierarchy
  • Subtle shadows: Soft depth instead of heavy borders
  • Natural flow: Clear reading progression
  • Professional simplicity: Clean, elegant, trustworthy
