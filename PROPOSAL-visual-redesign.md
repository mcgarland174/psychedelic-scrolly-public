- Visual weight competes with content

## RECOMMENDED APPROACH

**For Practitioner Economics:** Option A (Vertical Flow)
- Most logical reading progression
- Shows calculation clearly
- Professional without being boxy

**For Consumer Reasons:** Option B (Simple List)
- Cleanest, most elegant
- Easy to scan
- Professional and minimal

**For Access Statistics:** Option A (Single Stat Block)
- Presents the paradox clearly
- Minimal visual elements
- Context integrated well

---

## DESIGN PRINCIPLES FOR ALL

1. **Less is more** - Remove boxes where possible
2. **White space** - Let content breathe
3. **Typography hierarchy** - Use size and weight instead of borders
4. **Minimal color** - Stick to brand colors sparingly
5. **Natural flow** - Top to bottom, left to right
6. **Context integrated** - Don't isolate explanatory text in separate boxes
